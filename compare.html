How does Goal 10 and Goal 3 Relate to Each Other?

The Direct Relationship between the two

When reflecting on the relashionship between the two UNSDGs, the first thing that comes to mind is that the root of many of the issues that the UNSDG's stem from is inequalities. In the case of Global Health and Wellbeing (goal 3), the envoirnment in where one grows up in and the differences in opprotunities that they are provided play a signifigant role in their overall health. If the world is successfully able to accomplish the goals that Goal 10 (reducing inequalities) intends to do, then many of the issues facing the world with regards to health will also be solved as the issues originate from inequalities. Therefore, the relationship between the two goals is very important as it proivdes the world a chance to solve many of its challenges as effeftivly and effeciently as possible
